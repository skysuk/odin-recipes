Kimbap

Description

Kimbap is a Korean dish consisting of rice and various fillings such as egg, radish, spinach, and more. The whole thing is then wrapped in seaweed.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Seaweed sheet
  • Fillings of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Prep fillings
  3. Place seaweed sheet over bamboo mat
  4. Spread layer of rice over seaweed
  5. Spread layer of fillings over rice
  6. Tightly roll ingredients using bamboo mat
  7. Brush roll with sesame oil
  8. Cut roll into pieces of your choosing
